University Admissions Assistance

Before you even leave home to learn English, you can take full advantage of securing your direct admission to one of ELS's many partner colleges and universities in the USA. You can apply for direct admission for both undergraduate and graduate degree programs. Our Direct Admission service is for students who already meet the English language entry requirement for their chosen ELS partner college or university. If you don't meet the requirement yet, consider ELS Conditional Admission.

Our Direct Admission support offers you:

  • Support with selecting a college or university and completing the application process.
  • A full study plan that includes both English language studies and university plans before leaving your home country.
  • A smooth transition upon graduating with your ELS Certificate of Completion.
  • A university program start date for which semester you will begin your studies.
  • A chance to brush up on your English at one of our university campus-based centers, providing a smooth and supported transition into American student life.
